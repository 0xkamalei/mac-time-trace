Powerful Features

Automatic Time Tracking

Passively tracks all your app usage, websites, and documents in real-time without manual intervention. Seamlessly runs in the background while you focus on your work.

Intelligent Timeline View

Visualize your day with an intuitive timeline that automatically groups related activities. Zoom in/out to view different time ranges and get a complete picture of your productivity.

Advanced Project Management

Create unlimited projects and subprojects. Effortlessly assign time entries with drag-and-drop functionality. Set productivity scores for each project to track efficiency.

AI-Powered Insights

Our cutting-edge AI automatically summarizes your activities, groups related tasks, highlights key themes, and suggests accurate time entries to save you valuable time.

Calendar & Call Integration

Seamlessly integrates with your calendar and automatically detects calls and meetings. Import call records from iPhone and associate them with your timeline.

Team Collaboration

Share projects and time entries with your team while maintaining individual privacy. Administrators can view aggregated data without accessing personal information.

Smart Automation Rules

Create custom rules to automatically categorize activities. Simply hold the Option key while dragging to set up rules that save you from repetitive manual assignments.

Mobile & Web Integration

Access your time data anywhere with our web app. Start/stop timers on your phone and sync seamlessly with your Mac. Works as a PWA for a native-like experience.

About Time Vibe

Time Vibe is the next-generation time tracking solution for macOS, built as a powerful alternative to Timing App. Designed with professionals in mind, we help users gain deep insights into their time usage patterns to boost productivity and work-life balance.

Our team of engineers and time management experts have created an intuitive, intelligent tool that works seamlessly with your workflow. We believe that understanding how you spend your time is the first step to making meaningful improvements.

With advanced AI capabilities, automatic tracking, and a beautiful user interface, Time Vibe transforms time management from a chore into a powerful advantage for your professional success.
